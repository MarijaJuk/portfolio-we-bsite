About

My Story

Hi, I’m Marija Jukneviciute, I’m originally from Vilnius, Lithuania, living in Berlin for the past 6 years. Being an artist at heart and my favourite pastime (usually while making beautiful designs of course) is listening to music! My “Spotify Wrapped” revealed that in the year 2021 I spent more time listening to music than 97% of other listeners in Lithuania! I love the bands from the 60’s Boston Sound, space guitars, psychedelic folk, electronica, British Invasion..and so many more. I also love taking long walks a few times per week while listening to interesting podcasts as well as reading books. When I’m with my friends, I enjoy playing games - let it be a cozy evening at home playing the ‘’Risk’’ or the ‘’Ticket to Ride’’ or an evening in the bar playing table football. My friends also know that I won’t miss an opportunity to dance. I’ve been taking ballet and breakdance lessons when I was young and recently I attended 6-months courses for adults called ‘’Tanz als Beruf’’ (Dancing as a Job), where I practiced Ballet, Modern, Contemporary and Improvisation dance...because why not! My amazing 9-year old daughter also loves dancing and sometimes we like to go crazy when we need a break from her math homework. Before the Corona cloud covered the Planet Earth, I also traveled quite a lot. I was studying Graphic Design at Vilnius College of Technologies and Design when I was invited to work as a website administrator in a start-up company of someone I know. Since the past 10 years, I’ve been a part of a team, which developed several successful leisure related websites and apps. In my role, I helped to resolve usability issues, moderated user-generated content, and helped to improve internal administration systems. Additionally, I provided customer support to users, processed emails and complaints. The desire to create user-oriented products led me to the field of UI design. I want to make design solutions for creative industries, such as music, film, and fashion because I was always interested in products, that make a social statement as well as stimulate the mind.
